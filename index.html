About the Show

Knights of the Grill is the undisputed heavy hitter of Chad TV, a high-octane culinary spectacle where the sizzle of the charcoal is matched only by the intensity of the "bro-ship." Hosted by the legendary Lewis and his squad of elite grill-masters, the show is hailed as the greatest of all time because it ignores trivialities like "exact portions" or "balanced flavors" in favor of what truly matters: pure, unadulterated vibes and jawlines sharp enough to cut onions. It is a masterclass in backyard dominance that proves the secret to a perfect meal is not a recipe; it is the raw, chaotic energy of a man who treats a burger flip like a championship-winning play and every challenge a thing to mog.
